# Training Plan: Jun–Sept 2026

**Why:** Covers the O'Fallon → The Hague move transition. Road cycling ends Jul 6 (bike ships); running becomes the primary structured sport through the move and early Netherlands period. ERAU Centennial Run Challenge (100 mi, Jun 12–Sep 20) provides structure and accountability for the running build.

## How to apply

When planning training weeks, load the relevant phase template. Dutch bike sessions are active transport, not fitness — they contribute minimal training stress. Wind sessions are wild-card volume; adjust next-day intensity accordingly. Cannondale arrival date is a 4-week window (Aug 17–Sep 13) — treat road cycling as "available when confirmed."
