▸ Dual bindings: Several buttons show two functions — ED uses Primary/Secondary bindings contextually (e.g., flight vs UI panel mode). The main function is shown first, secondary below the dashed line.

▸ Left twist = Ahead Thrust (direct thrust, not autothrottle). Speed management is button-only via 0%/50%/100% presets on the top row. Twist spring-centers to zero — ideal for FA-off.

▸ FSD buttons: Btn 14 = supercruise (short/local). Btn 19 = hyperspace jump (long/next system). Mnemonic: short trip left, long trip right.

▸ Pip management: SYS for shields under fire, ENG for FA-off maneuvering. WEP remains on keyboard for now. Balance/Reset pips to equal (2-2-2) is the "Balance Power Distribution" binding in ED — keep that on keyboard or add to a future slide switch mode.

▸ 4-way slide switch is unbound — reserved for future mode layers if more bindings are needed (combat countermeasures, WEP pips, DSS, etc.).

▸ Boost (Btn 20): Essential even for non-combat — submit-boost-boost-jump is the standard interdiction escape. Also the foundation of FA-off maneuvering (boost-and-turn).

▸ Pitch axis is inverted (pull back = nose up).
